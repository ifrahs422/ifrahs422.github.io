How to Appeal Your Ticket

You can increase your chances of successfully challenging your issued ticket by following the steps below.

  1. Gather your ticket information
  2. Decide whether you want to contest the ticket in person or by mail
  3. If contesting the ticket in person follow the steps below:
    1. Schedule a hearing in person before 7 days of your ticket being issued has elapsed by calling, filling out the form on the website, or by mail.
      • You can call the City of Chicago's Ticket Helpline @ or (TTY - Hearing Impaired) and mention you want to schedule a hearing.
      • Alternatively, you can schedule a hearing online by clicking on the following link Submitting a Hearing Request and then clicking the "Get Started Online" button.
      • A third alternative is requesting an in-person hearing by filling out the in-person hearing bubble on the ticket envelope or returning the envelope that was given along with the Notice of Violation.
